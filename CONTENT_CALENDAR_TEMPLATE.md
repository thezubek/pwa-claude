## How to Use This Template

1. **Plan Monthly:** Review this template at the start of each month
2. **Batch Create:** Create content in batches (e.g., all Instagram posts for the week)
3. **Schedule Ahead:** Use scheduling tools (Later, Buffer) to automate posting
4. **Track Performance:** Note which content performs best and create more of it
5. **Stay Flexible:** Adjust based on trends, news, and performance

## Quick Reference: Optimal Posting Times

**Instagram:**
- Best days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- Best times: 11 AM - 1 PM, 7 PM - 9 PM EST
- Worst time: Late night, early morning

**Facebook:**
- Best days: Wednesday, Thursday, Friday
- Best times: 1 PM - 3 PM EST
- Worst time: Weekends

**Pinterest:**
- Best days: Saturday, Sunday
- Best times: 8 PM - 11 PM EST
- Note: Pinterest is evergreen, timing less critical

**TikTok:**
- Best days: Tuesday, Thursday, Friday
- Best times: 7 PM - 9 PM EST
- Note: Algorithm-driven, post consistently

**Email:**
- Best days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- Best times: 10 AM or 2 PM EST
- Worst time: Monday morning, Friday afternoon
