## üìã Executive Summary

This document outlines a comprehensive content strategy for HEVENTON.DE, a specialized online retailer focusing on high-quality women's blouses and men's shirts. The strategy aims to:

- **Increase organic traffic** through SEO-optimized educational content
- **Reduce return rates** with detailed size guides and product information
- **Build brand trust** through storytelling and transparency
- **Improve conversion rates** with styling inspiration and customer proof
- **Enhance customer satisfaction** with care guides and support content

## üé® Visual Content Guidelines

### Photography Style
- Clean, minimalist backgrounds (white, light gray, natural settings)
- Natural lighting preferred
- Mix of product-only and lifestyle shots
- Diverse models (age, body type, ethnicity)
- Authentic, not overly edited
- Close-ups showing fabric texture and quality

### Brand Voice & Tone
- **Professional yet approachable**
- **Knowledgeable but not condescending**
- **Confident in quality**
- **Helpful and educational**
- **Warm and inclusive**

**Do's:**
- Use "you" to speak directly to customers
- Be specific and detailed
- Focus on benefits, not just features
- Use active voice
- Break up text with headers and bullets

**Don'ts:**
- Don't use excessive jargon
- Avoid hype or exaggeration
- Don't talk down to customers
- Avoid generic statements

### Example Voice Application

**Generic:** "Our blouses are made from the best materials."

**HEVENTON Voice:** "We source 100% cotton from certified suppliers because we believe you deserve fabrics that breathe, last, and feel incredible against your skin."

## üéì Training & Guidelines

### Content Writer Guidelines

**Blog Post Structure:**
1. **Compelling Headline** (H1) - Include target keyword, 60 chars max
2. **Introduction** (150-200 words) - Hook, problem, solution preview
3. **Body** (1500-2000 words) - Break into H2/H3 sections, use bullets, short paragraphs
4. **Conclusion** (100-150 words) - Summarize key points
5. **Strong CTA** - Clear next step for reader

**Writing Tips:**
- Use short sentences (15-20 words average)
- Keep paragraphs to 3-4 sentences
- Use bullet points and numbered lists
- Add images every 300 words
- Include examples and specifics
- Write in active voice
- Use transition words
- Answer the "so what?" for every point

### Photography Guidelines

**Product Photos:**
- Clean white background
- Multiple angles (front, back, detail, lifestyle)
- Consistent lighting
- Show fabric texture in close-ups
- Include model shots with size information

**Lifestyle Photos:**
- Natural settings
- Diverse models
- Show products being worn/used
- Authentic scenarios
- Good lighting (golden hour for outdoor)

**Social Media Photos:**
- Square format (1:1) for feed
- Vertical (9:16) for stories/reels
- Consistent filter/editing style
- Mix of product and lifestyle
- User-generated content when possible
