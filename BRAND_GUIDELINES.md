## Brand Overview

### Mission Statement
To provide customers with unique, personalized, and sustainable home decoration products that transform their living spaces into reflections of their individual style and values.

### Vision Statement
To become the go-to destination for personalized home decor that combines quality, sustainability, and affordability, helping people create homes they love.

### Core Values

**1. Personalization**
Every home is unique, and our products should reflect that. We celebrate individuality and help customers express their personal style.

**2. Sustainability**
We're committed to reducing waste through made-to-order production and using eco-friendly materials whenever possible.

**3. Quality**
We never compromise on quality. Every product meets our high standards before reaching our customers.

**4. Accessibility**
Designer-quality decor should be accessible to everyone, not just those with unlimited budgets.

**5. Community**
We build genuine connections with our customers and celebrate their stories, homes, and lives.

### Typography

**Primary Typeface (Headings & Display)**

**Option 1: Playfair Display**
- Elegant, classic serif
- Use for: Headlines, featured quotes, logo
- Weights: Regular, Bold
- Where: Headings (H1, H2), hero text, elegant touchpoints

**Option 2: Montserrat**
- Clean, modern sans-serif
- Use for: Subheadings, labels, UI elements
- Weights: Light, Regular, Semi-Bold, Bold
- Where: H3-H6, buttons, navigation

**Body Typeface**

**Option: Open Sans**
- Highly readable sans-serif
- Use for: All body copy, captions, descriptions
- Weights: Regular, Semi-Bold
- Where: Paragraphs, product descriptions, emails

**Typography Scale**

| Element | Font | Size | Weight | Line Height |
|---------|------|------|--------|-------------|
| H1 | Playfair Display | 48px | Bold | 1.2 |
| H2 | Playfair Display | 36px | Bold | 1.3 |
| H3 | Montserrat | 24px | Semi-Bold | 1.4 |
| H4 | Montserrat | 20px | Semi-Bold | 1.4 |
| Body Large | Open Sans | 18px | Regular | 1.6 |
| Body | Open Sans | 16px | Regular | 1.6 |
| Body Small | Open Sans | 14px | Regular | 1.5 |
| Caption | Open Sans | 12px | Regular | 1.4 |
| Button | Montserrat | 16px | Semi-Bold | 1 |

**Typography Don'ts:**
- ‚ùå Don't use more than 3 fonts in one design
- ‚ùå Don't use all caps for long text (headlines only)
- ‚ùå Don't use decorative fonts for body text
- ‚ùå Don't reduce line height below 1.4 for body copy

### Iconography

**Icon Style:**
- Line icons (not filled)
- 2px stroke weight
- Rounded corners
- Minimal detail
- Consistent size (24px √ó 24px standard)

**Icon Color:**
- Primary: Sage Green or Terracotta
- On dark backgrounds: Cream
- In text: Charcoal

**Common Icons Needed:**
- Sustainability (leaf, recycle symbol)
- Customization (paint palette, magic wand)
- Shipping (truck, package)
- Quality (star, checkmark, badge)
- Social media (Instagram, Pinterest, Facebook, TikTok)

### Patterns & Textures

**Approved Patterns:**
- Subtle linen texture (backgrounds)
- Watercolor washes (accents, not overwhelming)
- Minimal geometric patterns (sparse)
- Natural wood grain (product shots)

**Pattern Usage:**
- Use at 20-30% opacity for backgrounds
- Never overpower main content
- Keep it subtle and sophisticated
- Ensure text remains readable

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice Characteristics

**Warm & Welcoming (Always)**
- Friendly and approachable
- Like talking to a design-savvy friend
- Never cold, corporate, or distant
- Use conversational language

**Inspiring & Creative**
- Encourage creativity and self-expression
- Share ideas and inspiration
- Celebrate beauty and design
- Use vivid, descriptive language

**Authentic & Transparent**
- Honest about our process and products
- Admit mistakes gracefully
- Share behind-the-scenes
- Real, not perfect

**Knowledgeable but Not Pretentious**
- Share expertise without being condescending
- Explain design concepts accessibly
- Help, don't lecture
- Make design feel achievable

**Sustainable & Conscious**
- Talk about sustainability naturally (not preachy)
- Focus on benefits, not guilt
- Celebrate conscious choices
- Be genuine, not greenwashing

### Tone Variations by Context

**Social Media (Casual, Friendly)**
- Use contractions (we're, you'll, can't)
- Emojis are welcome (but not excessive)
- Questions and engagement
- Exclamation points for excitement
- Example: "We're OBSESSED with how this turned out! üòç What do you think?"

**Product Descriptions (Helpful, Descriptive)**
- Focus on benefits and features
- Paint a picture of the product in use
- Address customer questions proactively
- Example: "Transform your living room into a cozy sanctuary with this ultra-soft fleece blanket."

**Email Marketing (Personal, Conversational)**
- Write like you're emailing a friend
- Use "you" and "your" frequently
- Storytelling when appropriate
- Clear calls-to-action
- Example: "Hey! We just launched our fall collection and thought of you..."

**Customer Service (Empathetic, Solution-Focused)**
- Acknowledge feelings and concerns
- Apologize sincerely when appropriate
- Focus on solutions
- Go above and beyond
- Example: "I'm so sorry your order arrived late. Let me make this right..."

**Blog Content (Educational, Inspiring)**
- Longer, more detailed explanations
- Story-driven when possible
- Mix of expertise and relatability
- Actionable takeaways
- Example: "You don't need a designer's budget to create a magazine-worthy space. Here's how..."

### Language Guidelines

**Words We Love:**
- Transform, create, personalize, unique
- Cozy, warm, inviting, beautiful
- Sustainable, conscious, eco-friendly
- Story, journey, expression
- Home, space, sanctuary, haven

**Words We Avoid:**
- Cheap, discount (use "affordable," "save")
- Perfect, flawless (use "beautiful," "stunning")
- Must-have, need (use "love," "recommend")
- Luxury, high-end (too pretentious)
- Revolutionary, groundbreaking (too salesy)

**Inclusive Language:**
- ‚úÖ "Partner" instead of "husband/wife" when general
- ‚úÖ "They/them" for generic pronouns
- ‚úÖ "People" or "customers" instead of "guys"
- ‚úÖ Show diverse homes and families
- ‚úÖ Accessible pricing language (not "cheap" or "budget")

### Grammar & Style Preferences

**General Rules:**
- Use Oxford comma (always)
- Contractions are fine (and encouraged)
- Sentence case for headlines (not title case)
- Numbers: Spell out one through nine, use numerals for 10+
- Dates: Month Day, Year (November 18, 2025)
- Percentage: Use % symbol (not "percent")

**Punctuation:**
- Exclamation points: Use sparingly, with purpose
- Emojis: 1-3 per social post maximum
- Ellipses: Avoid (seems uncertain)
- Ampersand (&): Only in design elements, not body copy

## Photography Guidelines

### Photography Style

**Overall Aesthetic:**
- Bright and airy
- Natural lighting preferred
- Minimalist styling
- Clean backgrounds
- Warm tones
- Aspirational but achievable

**Lighting:**
- Natural light whenever possible
- Soft shadows
- Golden hour for lifestyle shots
- Avoid harsh overhead fluorescent
- Bright enough to see product details

**Composition:**
- Rule of thirds
- Negative space (don't overcrowd)
- Leading lines
- Depth and dimension
- Show scale (include context clues)

### Product Photography

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 2000px √ó 2000px
- White or lifestyle background
- Multiple angles
- Detail shots showing texture/quality
- Styled in realistic settings

**Product Photo Types:**

**1. Hero Shot (White Background)**
- Clean, professional
- Product centered
- Even lighting
- Sharp focus
- Use for: Main product image, ads, marketplace listings

**2. Lifestyle Shot (In-Home Setting)**
- Product in real room
- Styled with complementary items
- Shows scale and use
- Aspirational but relatable
- Use for: Instagram, Pinterest, website banners

**3. Detail Shot**
- Close-up of texture, print quality
- Shows craftsmanship
- Highlights unique features
- Use for: Product galleries, quality messaging

**4. Scale Shot**
- Product with reference object (person, furniture)
- Shows actual size
- Helps customer visualize
- Use for: Addressing size questions

**5. Customization Example**
- Shows personalization options
- Multiple variations
- Demonstrates possibilities
- Use for: Marketing customization features

### Lifestyle Photography

**Room Settings:**
- Tidy but lived-in (not sterile)
- Natural decor elements (plants, books, coffee)
- Cohesive color schemes
- Multiple style aesthetics (modern, boho, farmhouse)
- Diverse home types (apartments, houses, various sizes)

**People in Photos:**
- Candid, natural poses
- Diverse representation
- Interacting with products
- Partial shots often better than full face
- Focus on the home, people as supporting

**Prop Styling:**
- Minimal and intentional
- Complementary colors
- Natural materials (wood, plants, textiles)
- Coffee mugs, books, candles (hygge vibes)
- Avoid clutter or distraction from product

### Photo Editing Style

**Color Grading:**
- Warm, slightly desaturated
- Consistent preset across all photos
- Boost natural tones
- Slight vignette okay for mood
- Don't oversaturate

**Editing Checklist:**
- Brightness: Light and airy, not blown out
- Contrast: Subtle, maintain soft feel
- Saturation: -5 to -10 from original
- Temperature: Slightly warm
- Sharpness: Just enough for clarity
- Noise reduction: If needed for darker shots

**Consistency:**
- Use same editing preset for cohesive feed
- Maintain consistent brightness levels
- Similar color temperature across all images
- Recognizable visual style

### Photos to Avoid

**‚ùå Don't:**
- Dark, underexposed images
- Overly filtered or color-graded
- Cluttered, busy backgrounds
- Poor quality or blurry
- Stock photos that look generic
- Inconsistent lighting
- Products that look cheap or low-quality

## Social Media Guidelines

### Instagram

**Feed Aesthetic:**
- Cohesive color palette (warm neutrals, sage, terracotta)
- Mix of product shots, lifestyle, and UGC
- Consistent filter/editing
- Balanced grid layout
- No harsh contrast between posts

**Feed Grid Pattern (Every 9 Posts):**
- 4 lifestyle photos
- 3 product shots
- 2 UGC or inspirational content

**Stories Branding:**
- Consistent font (Montserrat for main text)
- Brand colors for backgrounds
- Logo watermark (subtle, bottom corner)
- Templates for recurring segments
- Maintain visual hierarchy

**Reels:**
- Vertical format (9:16)
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- On-brand music or trending sounds
- Text overlays in brand fonts
- End screen with CTA and logo

**Highlights:**
- Custom highlight covers (consistent style)
- Organized categories: Products, Tips, Reviews, About, Sales
- Update regularly

### Pinterest

**Pin Design:**
- Vertical orientation (2:3 ratio, 1000√ó1500px)
- Text overlay with clear benefit
- Brand colors
- High-quality product/lifestyle images
- Logo in corner
- Readability on mobile

**Pin Copy:**
- Keyword-rich titles
- Detailed descriptions (200-300 characters)
- Include relevant hashtags
- Clear call-to-action
- Price if promoting product

### TikTok

**Video Style:**
- Authentic, less polished than Instagram
- Fast-paced, engaging
- Trendy sounds and effects
- Show process and behind-the-scenes
- Text captions for accessibility

**Branding:**
- Subtle (not heavy-handed)
- Watermark optional
- Let content be the focus
- Personality over perfection

### Facebook

**Post Style:**
- Mix of links, photos, videos
- Longer captions okay
- More informational than Instagram
- Customer service focused
- Community building

## Brand Applications

### Packaging

**Shipping Materials:**
- Eco-friendly packaging (recycled, recyclable)
- Branded tissue paper or stickers
- Thank you card with personal touch
- Care instructions included
- Subtle branding (not overdone)

**Unboxing Experience:**
- Clean, organized
- Product protected well
- Surprise element (sticker, discount code for next order)
- Encouragement to share (#MyPersonalSpace)
- QR code to review or socials

### Website

**Homepage:**
- Hero section with lifestyle image
- Clear value proposition
- Featured products or collections
- Customer testimonials
- Instagram feed integration

**Product Pages:**
- Multiple high-quality images
- Clear, benefit-focused descriptions
- Customization options obvious
- Reviews prominently displayed
- Related products suggestions

**Overall Feel:**
- Clean, modern design
- Easy navigation
- Fast loading
- Mobile-optimized
- Cohesive with brand colors and fonts

### Email Design

**Template:**
- Header with logo and navigation
- Hero image or banner
- Single-column layout (mobile-friendly)
- Brand colors and fonts
- Clear CTAs (buttons in brand colors)
- Footer with social links and contact

**Subject Lines:**
- Emoji at start (when appropriate)
- Personalization when possible
- Clear benefit or curiosity
- Under 50 characters
- Avoid all caps or excessive punctuation

## Brand Evolution

### Review Schedule

- **Quarterly:** Review performance of visual content, adjust if needed
- **Bi-annually:** Review voice and messaging effectiveness
- **Annually:** Comprehensive brand audit and updates

### When to Evolve

**Consider updates when:**
- Target audience shifts
- Market trends change significantly
- Current branding feels outdated
- Business expands into new categories
- Customer feedback indicates confusion

**Keep consistent:**
- Core values
- Mission and vision
- Overall personality
- Recognition elements (if working well)
