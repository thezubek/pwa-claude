**Research Date:** November 18, 2025

## Executive Summary

The German home decoration wholesale market is well-established with several major players offering comprehensive B2B solutions. The market emphasizes sustainability, eco-friendly materials, and minimalist designs. Both traditional wholesale and modern dropshipping models are available for new retailers.

## 5. RECOMMENDED APPROACH

### For Starting Out (Low Risk):

**Option 1: Dropshipping**
- **Best Choice:** BigBuy or AppScenic
- **Why:** No inventory investment, no MOQ, fast EU shipping
- **Ideal for:** Testing the market, low initial capital

### For Growth (Medium Investment):

**Option 2: Traditional Wholesale**
- **Best Choice:** Wurm or BOLTZE
- **Why:** Better margins, reliable supply, marketing support
- **Ideal for:** Established business model, ready to invest ‚Ç¨1,000-5,000

### Hybrid Approach:
- Start with dropshipping to test products and market
- Identify bestsellers
- Transition bestsellers to wholesale inventory for better margins
- Keep unique/seasonal items on dropshipping

## 6. NEXT STEPS

1. **Register with Suppliers:**
   - Create B2B accounts with Wurm and BOLTZE
   - Sign up for BigBuy or AppScenic for dropshipping

2. **Request Catalogs:**
   - Download digital catalogs
   - Visit physical showrooms if possible (BOLTZE in Hamburg/Essen)

3. **Sample Orders:**
   - Order samples to assess quality
   - Test shipping times and packaging

4. **Market Research:**
   - Identify your target customer segment
   - Research competitor pricing
   - Define your unique positioning (sustainability, style, price point)

5. **Legal Requirements:**
   - Verify tax registration (VAT) for B2B purchases
   - Ensure business registration is complete
   - Check import/compliance requirements
