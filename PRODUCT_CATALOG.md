## Pricing Strategy

### Pricing Tiers

**Budget-Friendly** ($20-35)
- Small posters
- Standard mugs
- Coaster sets
- Small pillows (16x16")
- Single ornaments

**Mid-Range** ($35-75)
- Medium canvas prints
- Large throw pillows
- Framed prints (8x10, 11x14)
- Blankets (throw size)
- Decorative accessories

**Premium** ($75-160)
- Large canvas prints
- Multiple-piece gallery sets
- Metal/acrylic prints
- Queen-size blankets
- Framed prints (16x20+)

### Pricing Formula

**Standard Products:** POD Cost √ó 2.5-3.0 = Retail Price
**Premium Products:** POD Cost √ó 2.0-2.5 = Retail Price (higher base cost)
**Bundles/Sets:** Individual prices √ó quantity √ó 0.80-0.85 (15-20% savings)

### Psychological Pricing
- End prices in 8, 5, or 2 instead of 9 or 0
- Example: $48 instead of $49.99 or $50.00
- Creates perceived value while maintaining premium feel

## Product Photography Guidelines

### Image Requirements (Per Product)

**Minimum 5 Images:**
1. **Hero shot:** Product on clean white background
2. **Lifestyle shot:** Product styled in real room setting
3. **Detail shot:** Close-up of quality/texture
4. **Scale shot:** Product showing size context (with person or furniture)
5. **Customization example:** Showing personalization options

### Photography Style Guide

**Lighting**
- Natural, bright lighting
- Soft shadows
- No harsh artificial light
- Golden hour for lifestyle shots preferred

**Styling**
- Minimalist, clean backgrounds
- Complementary props (books, plants, coffee mugs)
- Neutral color palettes (whites, beiges, grays) with pops of color
- Show products in aspirational but achievable settings

**Angles**
- Straight-on for wall art
- 45-degree angle for pillows and accessories
- Overhead for flat lays
- Multiple angles to show dimension

**Consistency**
- Same style across all product photos
- Consistent color grading/filters
- Similar backgrounds and props
- Professional, cohesive look

### Model/Room Diversity
- Show products in different room styles (modern, boho, traditional)
- Diverse home settings (apartments, houses, various sizes)
- Inclusive representation in lifestyle shots

### Product Testing Protocol

**Before Full Launch:**
1. Order 2-3 samples of each product
2. Test quality (printing, materials, durability)
3. Photograph samples
4. Test customization process
5. Calculate accurate production times
6. Set final pricing

**Ongoing Testing:**
- Monthly: Order samples of new products
- Quarterly: Re-order bestsellers to verify consistent quality
- Annually: Review full product line and discontinue poor performers
