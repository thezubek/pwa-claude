## Paid Advertising Strategy

### Facebook & Instagram Ads

**Budget Allocation**
- Month 1-2: $500/month (testing phase)
- Month 3-6: $800-1,000/month (scaling winners)
- Month 7-12: $1,200-1,500/month (full scale)

**Campaign Structure**

**1. Awareness Campaigns (20% of budget)**
- Objective: Reach & Brand Awareness
- Audience: Broad interest targeting (home decor, interior design)
- Creative: Beautiful lifestyle imagery, brand introduction
- Format: Carousel ads, video ads

**2. Consideration Campaigns (30% of budget)**
- Objective: Traffic & Engagement
- Audience: Website visitors, video viewers, engaged users
- Creative: Product showcases, customer testimonials
- Format: Single image, collection ads

**3. Conversion Campaigns (50% of budget)**
- Objective: Purchases
- Audience: Add-to-cart abandoners, product viewers, lookalike audiences
- Creative: Product-focused with clear CTA, limited-time offers
- Format: Dynamic product ads, carousel ads

**Targeting Strategy**

**Interest-Based Targeting**
- Home decor enthusiasts
- Interior design
- Etsy shoppers
- Pinterest users
- Specific designers (Studio McGee, Joanna Gaines)
- Home improvement shows

**Lookalike Audiences**
- 1% lookalike of customer email list
- Website visitors (1% and 2%)
- Add-to-cart users
- Instagram engagers

**Retargeting Audiences**
- Website visitors (last 30 days)
- Product page viewers (specific products)
- Add-to-cart abandoners (last 7 days)
- Email subscribers (cross-channel)

**Ad Creative Best Practices**
- High-quality lifestyle images (not just product on white)
- Show products in styled settings
- Include people when possible (increases engagement 25%)
- Use video for 30% of ads (better performance)
- A/B test headlines, images, CTAs
- Mobile-first design
- Include social proof (reviews, ratings)

**Ad Copy Framework**
- Hook: Attention-grabbing opening (question, stat, pain point)
- Problem: Identify customer pain point
- Solution: How product solves the problem
- Proof: Social proof, testimonials, guarantees
- CTA: Clear next step with urgency

## Influencer & Partnership Marketing

### Influencer Strategy

**Micro-Influencers (Primary Focus)**
- Followers: 10K-50K
- Niche: Home decor, lifestyle, parenting
- Engagement rate: >3%
- Authentic, engaged audience

**Nano-Influencers (Supporting)**
- Followers: 1K-10K
- Highly engaged, niche communities
- Cost-effective, authentic

**Outreach Strategy**

**Step 1: Identify**
- Search hashtags: #homedecor #apartmenttherapy #homeinspo
- Use influencer platforms: AspireIQ, Upfluence
- Check engagement rates and audience demographics
- Verify authenticity (no fake followers)

**Step 2: Engage**
- Follow and engage with content for 2 weeks
- Leave thoughtful comments
- Share their content in Stories

**Step 3: Reach Out**
- Personalized DM or email
- Compliment specific content
- Explain brand and collaboration idea
- Be clear about compensation

**Collaboration Types**

**1. Product Gifting (Nano-influencers)**
- Send free products
- Ask for honest review (not guaranteed post)
- No payment required
- Good for brand awareness

**2. Sponsored Posts (Micro-influencers)**
- Payment: $150-500 per post depending on following
- Deliverables: 1 feed post + 3 Stories
- Include discount code for tracking
- Rights to repurpose content

**3. Affiliate Partnerships (Ongoing)**
- Commission: 15-20% per sale
- Custom discount code
- Monthly payments
- Best for long-term relationships

**4. Brand Ambassadors (Top performers)**
- 3-6 month contracts
- Monthly content requirements
- Exclusive discount code
- First access to new products
- Higher commission rate (20-25%)

**Influencer Contract Essentials**
- Deliverables (number of posts, Stories, etc.)
- Timeline and posting dates
- Content requirements (hashtags, tags, messaging)
- Payment terms
- Usage rights for repurposing
- FTC disclosure requirements

**Target:**
- Quarter 1: 3-5 micro-influencer collaborations
- Quarter 2: 5-8 collaborations
- Quarter 3: 8-10 collaborations
- Quarter 4: 10-15 collaborations (holiday push)
