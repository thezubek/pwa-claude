## INVENTORY MANAGEMENT STRATEGY

### Made-to-Order vs. Ready-to-Ship

**Made-to-Order (40% of products):**
- Custom ceramics
- Personalized items
- Large mirrors
- Custom color requests

**Benefits:**
- No inventory holding costs
- Customization premium pricing
- Less waste
- Sustainable story

**Drawbacks:**
- 2-3 week lead time
- Can't do last-minute orders
- Production scheduling needed

---

**Ready-to-Ship (60% of products):**
- Candles
- Throw pillows (standard colors)
- Small planters
- Standard accessories

**Benefits:**
- Immediate gratification
- Impulse purchases
- Gift-giving friendly
- Higher volume potential

**Drawbacks:**
- Inventory costs
- Risk of overstock
- Storage needs
- Potential waste

---

### Reorder Points by Product

**Fast Movers (reorder when 20 units left):**
- Bestselling candles
- Popular pillow colors
- Standard planters
- Small accessories under $30

**Medium Movers (reorder when 10 units left):**
- Seasonal textiles
- Mirrors
- Larger ceramics
- Mid-range items $30-60

**Slow Movers (made-to-order or small batches):**
- Custom items
- Premium pieces over $75
- Experimental designs
- Limited editions

---

## PRICING STRATEGY MATRIX

### Price Tiers

**Entry Tier ($20-30) - 30% of SKUs:**
- Purpose: Traffic drivers, impulse buys
- Products: Small candles, pillow covers, mini planters
- Volume: High
- Marketing: "Treat yourself" messaging

**Core Tier ($30-60) - 50% of SKUs:**
- Purpose: Main revenue driver, gift-giving
- Products: Standard candles, pillows, planters, accessories
- Volume: Medium-high
- Marketing: "Perfect gift" messaging

**Premium Tier ($60-100) - 15% of SKUs:**
- Purpose: Brand elevation, statement pieces
- Products: Large mirrors, throws, ceramic sets
- Volume: Medium
- Marketing: "Investment pieces" messaging

**Luxury Tier ($100+) - 5% of SKUs:**
- Purpose: Prestige, special occasions
- Products: Custom large pieces, limited editions
- Volume: Low
- Marketing: "Heirloom quality" messaging

---

### Bundle Pricing

**Gift Sets:**
- 2 items: 10% off
- 3 items: 15% off
- 4+ items: 20% off

**Examples:**
- "Cozy Night In" bundle: 2 candles + throw = $95 (reg $122)
- "New Home" bundle: Planter + pillow + candle = $85 (reg $104)
- "Spa Day" bundle: Bath tray + soap dish + candle = $78 (reg $94)

**Benefits:**
- Higher average order value
- Moves slower inventory
- Gift-ready packaging
- Increases perceived value

---

### Seasonal Pricing

**Pre-Season (20% premium):**
- Early access to new collections
- Limited quantities
- For loyal customers/email list

**Peak Season (full price):**
- Standard retail pricing
- No discounts except bundles
- Focus on value and quality

**Post-Season (30-50% off):**
- Clear seasonal inventory
- Make room for new collections
- Acquire new customers at lower risk

## RISK MITIGATION

### Production Risks

**Risk:** Supplier delays
**Mitigation:**
- Multiple supplier options
- Order 4-6 weeks before needed
- Build relationships with local makers
- Keep safety stock of fast movers

**Risk:** Quality issues
**Mitigation:**
- Sample all products before bulk orders
- Clear quality specifications in contracts
- Inspection process before shipping
- Easy return policy for defects

---

### Market Risks

**Risk:** Trends change faster than expected
**Mitigation:**
- 70% evergreen, 30% trendy mix
- Small batch testing of new items
- Stay flexible with production
- Monitor social media trends weekly

**Risk:** Oversaturation in candles/pillows
**Mitigation:**
- Strong differentiation (sustainability, handmade)
- Unique designs and colors
- Personalization options
- Build brand story and community

---

### Financial Risks

**Risk:** Cash flow problems
**Mitigation:**
- Start small, scale gradually
- Pre-orders for expensive items
- Payment plans with suppliers
- Emergency fund (3 months expenses)

**Risk:** Pricing too low
**Mitigation:**
- Factor in ALL costs (materials, labor, shipping, fees, marketing)
- Review pricing quarterly
- Don't compete on price alone
- Premium positioning with quality
