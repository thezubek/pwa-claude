## ETSY STRATEGY (MARKETPLACE VALIDATION)

**Why Etsy:**
- Built-in audience of handmade seekers
- Instant credibility for artisan products
- Lower customer acquisition cost
- Review system builds trust
- Mobile shopping optimized

**Etsy Fees to Factor:**
- Listing fee: $0.20 per item
- Transaction fee: 6.5% of sale price
- Payment processing: 3% + $0.25
- Offsite Ads: 15% (on sales from Etsy ads)
- **Total: ~10-25% of sale depending on ads**

---

### Etsy Shop Optimization

**Shop Setup:**
- Professional shop banner (3360x840px)
- Clear shop logo
- Complete About section (brand story)
- Shop policies (returns, shipping, etc.)
- FAQ section

**Product Listings:**
- 10 high-quality photos per listing
- First photo: clean product on white background
- Subsequent photos: styled, different angles, scale reference
- Keyword-rich titles (140 characters)
- Detailed descriptions (5,000 characters available)
- All 13 tags used (optimize for search)
- Clear variations (size, color)
- Competitive pricing

**Example Listing Title:**
"Handmade Soy Candle | Pine Scent | Eco-Friendly Winter Candle | Sustainable Home Decor | Christmas Gift | 8oz Glass Jar | Carbon Neutral"

**Etsy SEO Strategy:**
- Research top keywords (Etsy search bar, eRank tool)
- Use long-tail keywords in titles and tags
- Update listings quarterly for freshness
- Optimize for seasonal searches
- Monitor Etsy stats for search terms bringing traffic

---

### Etsy Success Tactics

**1. Star Seller Program (aim for this badge):**
- 95%+ on-time shipping
- 95%+ message response rate (within 24 hours)
- 4.8+ star average reviews
- $300+ in sales + 5+ orders in past 3 months

**2. Review Generation:**
- Include thank you note asking for review
- Follow up message 1 week after delivery
- Respond to all reviews (positive and negative)
- Feature reviews on Instagram

**3. Etsy Ads (Optional - $300-500/month):**
- Start with $10/day budget
- Focus on bestsellers
- Monitor ROAS (return on ad spend)
- Aim for 3:1 or better

**4. Sales and Promotions:**
- Free shipping over $35 (Etsy promotes this!)
- 10% off 2+ items
- Holiday sale events (Black Friday, etc.)
- Coupon codes for email subscribers

- Sustainable living advocates

---

**Outreach Strategy:**

**1. Identify (create list of 50-100)**
- Follow relevant hashtags: #homedecor #sustainablehome #slowliving
- Check follower count and engagement rate
- Review content quality and aesthetic fit
- Verify authentic following (not bought followers)

**2. Engage (2-4 weeks before outreach)**
- Like and comment on their posts
- Share their content to your Stories
- Build genuine relationship
- Understand their content style

**3. Outreach (personalized DM or email)**

*Template:*
```
Hi [Name]!

I've been following your beautiful home content for a while now, and I especially loved your recent post about [specific post].

I'm the founder of [YourBrand], and we create handmade, sustainable home decor. I thought our [product] would fit perfectly with your [aesthetic/values].

Looking forward to connecting!
[Your Name]
```

**Gifting (for nano/micro influencers <20k):**
- Send 2-3 products

- Provide discount code for their audience (they earn commission)

**Paid Partnerships (for 20k-50k influencers):**
- $200-500 per post depending on following
- 1 feed post + 3-5 Stories
- Usage rights for your marketing
- Discount code (track sales)
- Authentic voice (not overly scripted)

## BRAND VOICE & MESSAGING GUIDE

### Brand Voice Attributes

**Warm:** Like a knowledgeable friend sharing recommendations
**Knowledgeable:** Expertise in home decor, sustainability, design
**Authentic:** Real, honest, not salesy
**Inspiring:** Helping customers create homes they love
**Conscious:** Thoughtful about impact and choices

---

### Do's and Don'ts

**Do:**
- Use "we" and "you" (conversational)
- Share the "why" behind products
- Celebrate imperfections (handmade charm)
- Acknowledge price vs. value
- Use active voice
- Tell stories
- Ask questions
- Show, don't just tell

**Don't:**
- Use jargon or overly formal language
- Be preachy about sustainability
- Oversell or use hype
- Compare directly to competitors
- Make claims you can't back up
- Use all caps or excessive punctuation
- Copy others' content or voice

---

- "Supporting artisans, celebrating craftsmanship"

**3. Accessible Luxury**
- "Premium quality, honest pricing"
- "Looks expensive, priced fairly"
- "Investment pieces you'll love for years"

## CRISIS MANAGEMENT & REPUTATION

### Potential Issues & Responses

**Shipping Delays:**
- Proactive communication (email before they ask)
- Offer discount on future order
- Take responsibility, explain situation
- Provide tracking and updates

**Product Defect:**
- Apologize sincerely
- Immediate replacement or refund (customer choice)
- Prepaid return label
- Include small gift with replacement
- Follow up to ensure satisfaction

**Negative Review:**
- Respond within 24 hours
- Thank them for feedback
- Apologize for experience
- Offer to make it right (publicly)
- Take conversation to private DM/email
- Follow up when resolved

**Sustainability Criticism:**
- Acknowledge concern
- Be transparent about current practices
- Share what you're working on improving
- Don't be defensive
- Invite dialogue

---

### Review Management

**Encouraging Reviews:**
- Ask in post-purchase email (day 7-10)
- Include review link in packaging
- Incentivize: Enter to win monthly giveaway
- Feature reviews on social media
- Make it easy (direct links)

**Responding to Reviews:**
- Positive reviews: Thank them, show appreciation
- Neutral reviews: Address concerns, invite further conversation
- Negative reviews: See crisis management above
- All reviews: Respond within 48 hours
